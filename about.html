About Us

I am the proud owner of this cafe, located in the heart of Nagamachi-minami. I have a passion for coffee making and latte art, and I have been fortunate enough to win several awards for my skills.

I am passionate about making the perfect cup of coffee for my customers. I use only the highest quality coffee beans, and I roast them myself in-house. I also take great pride in my latte art skills. I love creating beautiful and unique designs for my customers.

I am grateful for the opportunity to share my passion for coffee with the people of Nagamachi-minami. I hope that you will come and visit my cafe soon, so that I can make you a delicious cup of coffee.
