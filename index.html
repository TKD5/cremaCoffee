ごん君さん

A cute kitten was painted on the wall. The lunch set menu was available until 4:00 p.m., so I decided to go there. There is a parking lot for one car in front of the restaurant and two other parking lots in other locations. The sandwich arrived first, with plenty of lettuce and a delicious bacon sandwich, which was very satisfying. The hojicha white mocha arrived a little later, with a very cute kitten on it...even an old man like myself can't help but smile when I see such a cute drink. The sandwiches were delicious, but the latte art skills of the waitress here were amazing.
